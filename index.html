A full-stack software engineer.

I'm a full stack software engineer who is extremely passionate about user experience. I've always greatly enjoyed HTML & CSS, they just need a better PR team. I enjoy working with React/Redux, Angular, and a mixture of other front end frameworks.

An efficient & well-tested backend goes hand in hand with an excellent client experience. I'm extremely excited by Node's possibilities & love using Express to make clean servers & RESTful APIs. Designing SQL & NoSQL database schemas is one of my favorite tasks, as it requires you to take in a variety of scaling & efficiency problems into account and try to produce the most optimized result possible.

When I'm not coding I'm a major news fan, and I'm always down to talk about foreign policy, politics, or economics. I also really enjoy walking, so don't be surprised if you see me strolling about with headphones on.
